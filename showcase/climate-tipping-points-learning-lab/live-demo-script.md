## Expected Questions And Answers

### Is this just a prompt pack?

No. The prompts are the surface. The deeper asset is the structured skill layer: metadata, evidence ratings, input schemas, output schemas, chaining hints, and interaction protocols.

### Can this scale technically?

Yes. The skill registry can drive routing, the context engine can inject class and student data, and Domain 20 interaction events can feed a teacher dashboard. The prototype path is straightforward.

### Does this replace teachers?

No. It makes teacher judgement more informed. The teacher remains the curriculum guardian, evidence interpreter, and final assessor.

### How do you prevent students from becoming dependent on AI?

The student-facing skills require retrieval before explanation, progressive hints instead of answers, teach-back, transfer, and unassisted checkpoints. Scaffolded and independent evidence are kept separate.

### Why climate tipping points?

Because it is a high-difficulty test case: complex systems, scientific uncertainty, emotional weight, misinformation risk, and genuine civic relevance.
