Four-week learning lab blueprint

Built for Year 12, IB, A-level, AP, or first-year undergraduate learners.

Enduring understandings

Backwards design spine
  1. Climate tipping points are nonlinear system changes where feedback loops can make gradual pressure produce sudden shifts.
  2. Scientific uncertainty describes probability, confidence, timescale, and model limits, not ignorance.
  3. AI accelerates learning only when students verify claims and preserve their own cognitive work.
  4. Responsible action requires bounded agency: control, influence, concern, and adult responsibility.

Final task

Systems brief

Students create and defend a Climate Tipping Point Systems Brief for a real school or community audience.

  • Feedback-loop explanation
  • Evidence from credible sources
  • AI audit trail
  • Bounded action recommendation
  • Unassisted performance reflection
